Tribute to Akira Toriyama

About Akira Toriyama

Toriyama Akira was a legendary Japanese manga artist, character designer, and storyteller, best known worldwide as the creator of the iconic manga Dragon Ball.

Achievement

  • Shogakukan Manga Award (1981) – for Dr. Slump
  • Created one of the best-selling and most influential manga of all time.
  • Chevalier of the French Order of Arts and Letters (2019) – for cultural impact.
